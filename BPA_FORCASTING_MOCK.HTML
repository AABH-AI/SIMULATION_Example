Coefficient of Variation (Demand)
Coefficient of Variation (CV)
Measures how much demand fluctuates relative to its average.
CV = Standard Deviation ÷ Mean
How to interpret:
  • Low CV (<50%) → Stable and consistent demand
  • High CV (≥50%) → Irregular and unpredictable demand
Use this to identify whether demand is steady or highly variable over time.
Consistent Demand :- Occurrence > 50% & Coefficient of Variation < 50%
Intermittent Demand :- Occurrence < 50% & Coefficient of Variation < 50%
Erratic Demand :- Occurrence >= 50% & Coefficient of Variation >= 50%
Lumpy Demand :- Occurrence < 50% & Coefficient of Variation >= 50%
